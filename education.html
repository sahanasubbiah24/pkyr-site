Why Civic Education Matters

From knowing your rights during police encounters to understanding how to register to vote, civic education gives you the tools to protect yourself, advocate for your community, and participate fully in democracy.

Register to Vote

Why Your Vote Matters

Voting is one of the most powerful tools you have to shape your community and your future. Every election—from local school boards to federal offices—affects your daily life.

Voting Requirements

  • Be a U.S. citizen
  • Meet your state's residency requirements
  • Be 18 years old on or before Election Day
  • Register to vote (deadlines vary by state)

How to Register

  1. Check your state's registration deadline
  2. Gather required documents (ID, proof of address)
  3. Complete registration online, by mail, or in person
  4. Confirm your registration status
